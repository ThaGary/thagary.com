About Me

My name is Gary! I'm currently learning code at Galvanize. I am from Galesburg IL aka a cornfield. I am a USN Veteran, I was honorably discharged as EM2(SW). I was stationed in Guam for 4 years but spent most of my time out at sea and in various countries in Asia. I like to snowboard, hike, and play with dogs. I have always been interested with code ever since I customized my Myspace page for the first time. I spend a lot of my free time on Twitch doing Community/Event Moderation. I have a verified TwitchBot (which means I can send 7500 messages in chat every 30 seconds) and I have a few scripts running in many chats across the platform with the help of my good friend Techno. I learned how to add my Twitch account to a script to connect with the chat client so that my account would automatically send thank you messages to new channel subscribers and donators for broadcasters. My goal is to work full time for a gaming company as a member of their web/software team.
